What is SCBypass?

SCBypass is a secure, fast web proxy / unblocker that lets you bypass almost any filter — whether it's school Chromebooks, network-level blocks, government censorship, or browser extensions.
Access YouTube, Discord, GeForce NOW, Spotify, Reddit, Instagram — and basically anything else — with strong evasion and privacy focus.
