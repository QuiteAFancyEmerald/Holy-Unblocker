I am getting an error relating to the IDB Database

{{mask}}{{ This typically happens whenever we do updates to our project. Simply click on the (i) icon in the top left corner of your browser (or lock symbol) and select "Clear Site Data" or "Clear Cookies and Site Data > Manage On-Site Data > Trash Icon". This will clear the IndexedDB database and allow you to use the proxy again. }}
